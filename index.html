About Me

I am a born and raised Utahn currently living in Sandy, Utah. I graduated from Brighton High School in 2002 and attended the University of Utah. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Political Science. I then completed a master's degree in Elementary Education.

I have been teaching for the past 11 years at Monte Vista Elementary in South Jordan, Utah. I taught 6th grade for 7 years and 5th grade in the Dual Language Immersion program during the last 5 years.

Soccer is my passion. I'm an avid supporter of Liverpool FC, and the English Premier League in general. When it comes to playing soccer, most of my teammates always mention at the end of the game, "great running". I claim that as my biggest strength and anything else you get from me is a bonus. I also enjoy playing or watching any other sport.
